2.6.1.1.1. Renesas RZ/V2H Camera Receiver Unit 64-bit packed pixel formats¶

2.6.1.1.1.1. 描述¶

These pixel formats are some of the RAW outputs for the Camera Receiver Unit in the Renesas RZ/V2H SoC. They are raw formats which pack pixels contiguously into 64-bit units, with the 4 or 8 most significant bits padded.
